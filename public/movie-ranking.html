The Best Movies According to Subhra


My top 3 favourite movies of all time

3 Idiots

The POV towards life shown in this movie really strikes deep into my thoughts every time I rewatch it.
“Ye jo apna dil hai na, bada darpok hai yar.. isko Bewakoof banake rakho.. life mein kitni bhi badi problem ho na, isko bolo koi baat nahi chachu sab thik thak hai…. Aal iz well, Aal iz well…!!!!”- Rancho.

Chhichhore

An ode to college-time friendships, romances, ragging, fights, competitions and countless memories, Chhichhore is a riot of emotions, and takes you on a nostalgic ride.
The movie basically conveys the message-
"In life's battles between winning and losing, the most important thing is 'life' itself"

Rang De Basanti

The movie itself is full ride of emotions, drama, laughter and sense of patriotism. The dialogues are nail-biting and thought provoking
Zindagi jeene ke do hi tarike hote hai… Ek jo ho raha hai hone do, bardaasht karte jao. Ya phir zimmedari uthao usse badalne ki!
College di gate de is taraf hum life ko nachate hai…Te dooji taraf life humko nachati hai

Jaane Tu... Ya Jaane Na

The story and plot of this Rom-Com is really engagine and entertaining.The preesentation of each and every cast was perfect in my eye.
Ek arse ke baad jab log milte hai toh ... har mulaqat mein ek choti si kahani hoti hai

Purani Jeans

Though poorly recognised movie but the story felt relatable with mine a little bit! Two of my fav dialogues from this movie are-
Zindagi mein dost toh kahi aate hai ... lekin purane dost us jeans ki tarah hote hai, jisse pehente hi you become free ... free to be yourself
Dosti na guitar ke strings ki tarah hoti hai ... ek baar tuning theek beth jaaye na ... har ek taar important hota hai
